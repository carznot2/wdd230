White Space

Telus

www.telus.com

I like that it has white space between the graphics and the text, and that the graphics are interesting and make it friendly at the same time. They use lots of space between graphics, margins, and the text. It makes it a friendly site to visit and is certainly not cluttered! It is a clean site design.

Visual Hierarchy

Air Canada

www.aircanada.com

For different users this website has more important features for some users, than for others. I might be interested in arranging a flight, or I could be simply interested in planning my next vacation and the website caters to both. It is also not cluttered which I like.
